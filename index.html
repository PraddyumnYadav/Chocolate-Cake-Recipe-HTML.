Chocolate Cake Recipe

Here is a fast and easy version of the Classic Chocolate Recipe. Kids Will Definately Love It Enjoy!

Difficulty Preperation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Dark chocolate: 200g: Rs. 1,99

Butter: 200g: Rs. 25

Sugar: 150g: Rs. 89

Flour: 80g: Rs. 35

Eggs: 4: Rs. 28

Instructions

  1. Melt the chocolate and the butter together
  2. Mix the sugar, eggs and the flour
  3. Mix everything to get a smooth dough
  4. Bake in the oven
